Founded in 2012, Canva originated as a simpler alternative to Photoshop, allowing students and professionals to create graphic design illustrations without a steep learning curve. Though Canva is mostly used for promotional design (it's very popular among librarians), it does feature an array of UX design tools.

Canva's basic features - its templates, stock photos, and editing and design features, are free to use, while its AI features and more business-friendly elements are part of paid plans, often provided by organizations.
